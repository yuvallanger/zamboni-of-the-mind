htop, kills, and collateral damage

Posted on August 19, 2014

htop (official website, github repository, wikipedia article) is a great tool used to list, sort, search, and manage processes. It is similar to top (official website, sourceforge profile, wikipedia article) but with many more features.

One of the more useful features of this program is being able to tag processes and sending all of the tagged processes a signal of your choosing. It will also ask for a confirmation before the signal is sent.
